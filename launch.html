A structured, week-by-week plan to get your first SKU live on Amazon — GTM strategy, India-side export compliance, and US setup, handled by operators who've built this exact playbook before.

What gets in the way

The problems first-time launchers run into

Different founders hit these in a different order — but almost everyone hits all six.

🎯

GTM & launch planning

No plan for what to launch or at what price — decisions made on gut feel, not consumer or competitor data.

FIXGTM models built from 100+ brand launches: SKU selection, seasonal planning, unit economics — before a unit ships.

🚢

Logistics & freight

Air vs. sea trade-offs unknown; no idea who the reliable forwarders are or how to read a freight quote.

FIXForwarder shortlist, FBA prep checklist, cargo insurance & tracking SOP — the freight playbook, done once.

💱

Money & banking

Amazon US needs a US bank account; FX spread on wires quietly costs 1.5–2% on every payout.

FIXUS entity setup guide, Mercury/Wise/Payoneer selection, and an RBI-compliant repatriation flow.

📋

Compliance & certs

Wrong HTS code means 10–25% excess duty; FCC/CPSC/FDA/Prop 65 requirements surface only after a listing gets flagged.

FIXA category compliance checklist covering India export docs and US import requirements, upfront.

📦

Amazon operations

Account verification failures delay launch by weeks; inventory forecasting is pure guesswork.

FIXAccount setup SOP, demand-planning templates, and account health monitoring from day one.

📈

Growth & scale

Post-launch plateau hits and there's no clear lever to pull when ranking stalls or ACOS spikes.

FIXA weekly operating rhythm plus a vetted vendor network across logistics, legal and marketing.
