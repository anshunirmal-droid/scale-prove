How it works

The Growth Diagnostic Framework

Every engagement starts with a structured audit across all 5 pillars — before any execution begins.

WEEK 1–2
Growth audit

Deep dive across all 5 pillars using your Seller Central data, ad reports and P&L. Output: a fix roadmap ranked by revenue impact.

MONTH 1
Quick wins

Fee reconciliation claims, negative-keyword harvesting, listing fixes, and reorder triggers — margin recovered immediately.

MONTH 2–3
Structural fixes

Campaign architecture, inventory model, per-SKU P&L and listing tests rebuilt — this is where the compounding work happens.

MONTH 4+
Growth retainer

Weekly operating rhythm, monthly business review, quarterly roadmap — new SKUs, geos and channels activated.
